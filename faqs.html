// Frequent Asked Questions

What are epoxy tables?

Epoxy tables are unique pieces of furniture created by combining epoxy resin with wood, resulting in an eye-catching and durable surface.

How are epoxy tables maintained?

Maintaining epoxy tables is easy. Simply wipe the surface regularly with a damp cloth to preserve its shine and cleanliness.

Can you create custom epoxy tables?

Yes, we craft epoxy tables to order, allowing you to get a product that perfectly matches your taste and needs.

How long does it take to make a custom epoxy table?

The crafting time varies depending on the table's design and size. Contact us for an estimate of the crafting time.

How can I place an order for my epoxy table?

You can contact us through the form on our website, via email, Facebook and Instagram pages or by phone to initiate the ordering process.

What are your shipping terms?

For Zagreb and the surrounding area of Zagreb, we offer free delivery. We also provide delivery services throughout Croatia and across Europe. Please contact us for delivery pricing

What types of wood do you use for making epoxy tables?

We use various types of wood based on our customers' preferences, including walnut, olive, cherry, ash, oak, and many others.

Can you make tables in different dimensions?

Yes, we craft tables in various dimensions to help you find the perfect piece for your space.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept only bank transfers and cash.

Where can I see more examples of your products?

On our Facebook and Instagram pages and TikTok
