// About Us

We Build Every Table From The Core Of Our Hearts


Welcome to Epoxy Tables NH, where craftsmanship meets creativity in the picturesque village of St. Ivan Zelina Tomaševec. Founded in the year 2021, Epoxy Tables NH is a bespoke epoxy table artisan atelier that thrives on turning your design visions into reality.
Located in the heart of a small, idyllic community, our passion for crafting exquisite epoxy tables has been the driving force behind our journey. We specialize in creating custom epoxy tables that are as unique as your imagination. Our commitment to quality craftsmanship, attention to detail, and a deep-rooted love for the artistry of epoxy resin define our work.

Whether you seek a captivating dining table, a conversation-starting coffee table, or any other epoxy table creation, we are here to bring your dreams to life. Each piece we create carries the hallmark of our dedication to perfection and is designed to make a statement in your space.

We invite you to explore our world of bespoke epoxy tables, where your ideas become tangible art.Thank you for being a part of our journey, and we look forward to crafting something exceptional for you.

Explore Some Of Our Epoxy Tables

Steamed Walnut Table

Crafted from beautifully steamed walnut and enriched with a touch of black epoxy resin, this table showcases exquisite dimensions of 250x100, with a substantial 4 cm thick tabletop. The sleek black powder-coated metal base adds a touch of sophistication to this piece.

Olive Wood Side Table

Another captivating olive wood side table from our workshop. The table measures 100x70 with a substantial 4 cm thick tabletop. It's gracefully supported by a black powder-coated metal base.

Walnut Wood Dining Table

An exquisite walnut dining table, intricately crafted with a fusion of black and translucent epoxy resin in a stunning 3D design, accentuating the raw, natural edges of the wood. The table boasts dimensions of 180 x 100, elegantly supported by a sleek black powder-coated metal base.

Olive Wood Coffee Table

Presenting our Olive Wood Coffee Table, beautifully handcrafted with a blend of olive wood and vibrant blue epoxy resin. This round table boasts a 60cm diameter, featuring a substantial 4cm thick tabletop and an overall height of 48cm. It rests elegantly on a black powder-coated metal base.

Oak Coffee Table

For a special client in Makarska, we crafted a distinctive oak coffee table, harmoniously blending with black translucent epoxy resin. The table takes the shape of a fig leaf, adorned with intricate veins on the surface, delicately filled with golden epoxy resin to create an even more captivating impression.

Mazera Wood Coffee Table

Introducing our Mazera Wood Living Room Coffee Table, measuring 100 x 60 cm. The tabletop, with a thickness of 3 cm, showcases the rich texture of mazera wood. The elegant metal base is coated in a lustrous gold finish, adding a touch of opulence to your living space.

Olive Wood Dining Table

Every one of our tables has a story, and this one has a hundred. Crafted from aged olive wood, over a century old, as if that weren't enough, we've combined it with transparent black epoxy resin to give it an even more luxurious appearance. We simply adore the combination of olive and epoxy resin. A lot of effort and time went into making this, but the result is truly one-of-a-kind. The table measures 180x90 in dimensions

// Our Projects

The Makarana Club Project

At Makarana Club in Makarska, we've gone above and beyond to curate a truly unique atmosphere. Our innovative tables are a masterpiece, handcrafted from a mesmerizing fusion of epoxy resin and shattered champagne bottles. These tables are more than just functional pieces; they're works of art designed to leave a lasting impression.

But the magic doesn't stop there. We've taken it a step further by embedding subtle LED lighting within these tables. This feature adds a captivating glow, ensuring that your experience at Makarana Club is nothing short of enchanting.




The Home Design Project

In our latest Home Design project, we've explored creative possibilities with epoxy resin tables, elevating the dining room and living room ambiance. Our aim was to craft spaces that blend elegance with contemporary design, and epoxy resin tables became the pivotal element in this unique concept.

In the dining room, where meals are shared and cherished moments with family and friends are created, we introduced a special touch. We opted for an epoxy resin dining table that combines functionality with striking aesthetics. Its transparent surface and interplay of light in the epoxy resin create a visually poetic centerpiece.

In the living room, the epoxy resin coffee table becomes an artistic statement. Its smooth surface, fusing the strength of wood with the transparency of epoxy resin, draws every eye. The fusion of natural elements and modern design strikes a harmonious balance, providing not just functionality but character to the space.
